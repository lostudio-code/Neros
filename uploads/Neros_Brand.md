BRAND POSITIONING
What is Neros?
Neros is not a drone company.
Neros is a sovereign defense manufacturing platform building the operating system for modern asymmetric warfare.
Neros vertically integrates design, manufacturing, software, electronics, radios, propulsion, and supply chain infrastructure to produce battlefield systems at industrial scale.
Their advantage isn’t merely hardware.
Their advantage is manufacturing velocity.

⸻

⸻

Mission
